Origins & Foundation

The Radha Krishna Divine Temple was established as a humble sanctuary devoted to the eternal love of Radha and Krishna. Over the decades, it flourished into a vibrant spiritual community, guided by the teachings of Bhakti Yoga and the timeless wisdom of the Vedic scriptures.

From the early days of small gatherings to grand festival celebrations, our temple remains a beacon for seekers wishing to cultivate devotion, humility, and compassion.
