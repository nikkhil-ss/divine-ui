Our Sacred Story

Founded in 1985, the Radha Krishna Divine Temple stands as a beacon of spiritual light, dedicated to preserving and sharing the eternal teachings of Vedic wisdom. What began as a small gathering of devotees has blossomed into a vibrant spiritual community.

The divine couple, Radha and Krishna, represent the highest expression of spiritual love. Their eternal pastimes in Vrindavan inspire us to cultivate pure devotion and selfless service to all beings.
