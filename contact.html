Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about visiting our temple

What are the temple visiting hours?

The temple is open daily from 4:30 AM to 9:00 PM. Different aartis and darshans are held throughout the day.

Is there accommodation available?

Yes, we have a guest house with clean rooms for pilgrims. Advance booking is recommended, especially during festivals.

Is prasadam available for all visitors?

Yes, free prasadam is served to all devotees and visitors daily. Special feasts are organized on weekends and festivals.

How can I book a ceremony at the temple?

You can contact our office during working hours (9 AM - 6 PM) or email us at events@radhakrishnatemple.org to book ceremonies.
